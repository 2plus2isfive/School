Aztec Civilization School Tour

Step back in time and immerse your students in the rich history of the Aztec Civilization with our exclusive educational tours. Our expertly guided tours provide a unique opportunity for students to explore the ancient ruins, learn about the Aztec culture, and understand the significance of their contributions to modern society. This hands-on experience is designed to complement classroom learning and ignite a passion for history in young minds. Our tours are meticulously planned to ensure a safe, engaging, and educational experience for all participants. From the majestic pyramids to the intricate stone carvings, students will witness the grandeur of the Aztec Empire firsthand. Our knowledgeable guides will share fascinating stories and insights, making history come alive. Join us for an unforgettable journey that will leave your students inspired and enriched.


Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer school field trips?

Yes, we offer school field trips. Depending on the time of year or other factors, the cost varies between $20 to $30 per student. We strive to provide an affordable and educational experience for all students.

When are tours available?

Tours are available at any time of year, however, it is recommended to go during the spring and summer seasons. These seasons offer the best weather conditions for exploring the ancient ruins and enjoying the outdoor activities.

How long does the tour last?

Our tours usually last one day, however there is a two day and a three day tour option for more in depth tours. The extended tours provide a more comprehensive experience, allowing students to delve deeper into the history and culture of the Aztec Civilization.

What should students bring on the tour?

Students should bring comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, a water bottle, and a notebook for taking notes. It is also advisable to bring a light jacket or sweater, as the weather can be unpredictable.

Are meals provided during the tour?

Yes, meals are provided during the tour. We offer a variety of options to accommodate different dietary needs. Our meal plans include vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options to ensure that all students can enjoy a nutritious meal.

Is there a discount for large groups?

Yes, we offer discounts for large groups. Please contact us for more information on group rates and discounts. We aim to make our tours accessible to as many students as possible, and we are happy to work with schools to accommodate their budgetary constraints.
