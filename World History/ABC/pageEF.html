Egypt

Egypt is a middle eastern country known for the pyramids of Giza. It is a predominantly a desert. It also has the Nile River, which is the longest river in the world at 4,132 miles long. The Nile River is a major source of water for Egypt.

Fascism

Fascism is a political ideology that gives all of the power to the government. It prioritizes success as a nation over happiness of citizens. The most known fascist leader was Adolf Hitler.
