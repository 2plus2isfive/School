U-Boats

German U-Boats were advanced submarines that caused a lot of trouble for the Allied Powers, as they were hard to counter. The name comes from the German word "Unterseeboot", meaning "Under Sea Boat".

Victory in Europe

Victory in Europe Day (V-E Day) is a day that celebrates the Allied victory over the Nazi's. It is every year on May 8th.
