ATHENS

GREECE



Name Background

Athens is named after the Greek Goddess of Wisdom, Athena. She was seen as a protector of Athens, and there is a statue dedicated to her.

Pictured: Statue of Athena



Type of Government

Athens was a democracy.


Who Held Power?

The citizens (refered to as 'demos' in this context) held political power.


Role of Citizens

Citizens participated in the government by voting. Military service was also required.


Similarities to Today

The United States of America has a similar democracy.


Purpose of Education

The purpose of educaton in Athens is the same as anywhere else. To learn.


School Attendees
